Caye Carline
About Us

Caye Carline means Carline's House in Haitian Creole, named after the owner Marie Carline. Marie was born and raised in Haiti. As a family-owned business, we take pride in caring for each and every diner. Our goal is to make everyone feel at home. Caye Carline strives to create a uniquely elevated experience of the flavors of traditional Haitian cuisine.
